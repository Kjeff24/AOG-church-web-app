DEPARTMENT MEN'S MINISTRY

This department is exclusively for married male members of the church. They meet every Mondays by 6pm in the church premises. They are led by a five man exco members, headed by the President. Their objectives include the following:

  • To walk daily in the spirit ⎵Gal. 5:16
  • To maintain Christian fellowship (Acts 2:42, Heb.10:25)
  • To serve in the house of God (Heb. 6:10, 2Tim. 2:20 ‑ 21)
WOMEN'S MINISTRY

This is the department of the married female members of the church. They meet every Mondays by 4pm to 6pm in the church premises. They are led by a seven person exco members (otherwise called Deaconess) headed by the President. Their objectives includes the following:

  • Not forsaking the assembling of the brethren (Heb. 10:25)
  • Giving in support of God's work (2Cor. 9:7).
  • Preaching the good news (Mark 16:15, Matt. 28:19).
  • Helping the needy (Acts. 2:45).
